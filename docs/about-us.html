Who we are

In India, millions of tons of food go to waste every year, even as millions of people struggle with hunger. According to estimates, nearly 40% of the food produced in the country is wasted due to inefficiencies in supply chains, improper storage, and excess consumption. This wastage not only contributes to food insecurity but also strains our natural resources like water, land, and energy, which are heavily used in food production. By redirecting surplus food to those in need, we can significantly reduce waste, ease the burden on the environment, and channel our resources toward more sustainable initiatives such as education, healthcare, and poverty alleviation. Preventing food wastage isn’t just about saving food—it’s about creating a more efficient and equitable society.

We are a community-driven initiative dedicated to reducing food waste and helping those in need. Our smart food storage units, distributed across the city, provide a simple and effective way for individuals and businesses to donate surplus food. Through our platform, donors can easily locate the nearest storage unit and contribute, ensuring that underprivileged and homeless individuals have access to fresh and nutritious meals. Together, we are building a more compassionate and sustainable future—one meal at a time.
