FAQS

How does food donation work?
We connect food donors with local distribution centers. Donors can schedule pickups through our platform, and our volunteers ensure the food reaches those in need.
What types of food are accepted?
We accept non-perishable foods, fresh produce, and prepared meals that meet safety standards. All donations must be unexpired and in good condition.
How can I volunteer?
You can sign up as a volunteer through our website. We have various roles including food collection, sorting, distribution, and administrative support.
How are my donations used?
Financial donations help us maintain our operations, including transportation costs, storage facilities, and technology infrastructure to connect donors with recipients.
Are donations tax-deductible?
Yes, we are a registered nonprofit organization and all donations are tax-deductible. You will receive a receipt for your donation for tax purposes.
How do I request food assistance?
If you or someone you know needs food assistance, please fill out the form on our website or contact us directly. We'll connect you with the nearest distribution center.
